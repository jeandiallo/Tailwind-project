Forwards

Football forwards are attacking players primarily responsible for scoring goals and creating offensive opportunities for their team.

Midfielders

Football midfielders are versatile players who control the flow of the game, linking defense and attack with vision, passing, and tactical awareness.

Defenders

Football defenders are responsible for preventing the opposing team from scoring by blocking attacks, intercepting passes, and maintaining defensive organization.

GoalKeepers

Football goalkeepers are the last line of defense, responsible for protecting the goal, making crucial saves, and organizing the defense.
